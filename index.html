Do I Want to Eat There?

GitHub | Live Site
A Civic Houston Hackathon submission, fine-tuned with improved functionality later by me and my friend JJ Spetseris. Sourced from the City of Houston's FY 2016 report data, there were originally over 56,000 entries with little or no documentation. We loaded the data into a database, and performed several passes at analyzing what each entry meant, and trying to eliminate false data and unimportant results. Our conclusions led to a cleaned up dataset of less than 14,000 restaurant entries and their most recent inspection score.
